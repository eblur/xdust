xdust extinction
================

Extinction (scattering and absorption) is computed through the
:class:`xdust.grainpop.SingleGrainPop` class.  After calling
``SingleGrainPop.calculate_ext()``, the following optical depth arrays are
populated:

- ``tau_ext`` — total extinction optical depth
- ``tau_sca`` — scattering optical depth
- ``tau_abs`` — absorption optical depth

See :doc:`grainpop` for full API details.
